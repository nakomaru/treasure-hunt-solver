Treasure Hunt Solver

Prizes still in play
Set the prizes in play, mark tiles as you flip them, and the board re-solves itself — telling you the best tile to flip next.
Flipped an empty tile? Left-click it to mark it Miss ✕.
Found a prize? Click or drag its shape from the prize card onto the board. It snaps in as ✓, correcting any marks underneath, and updates the count. Click a ✓ tile to undo.
Double-click or right-click a tile to mark it Hit ● — for when you've uncovered a prize but aren't sure of its adjacent tiles.
Each tile shows its chance of hiding a prize. White outline ★ = best flip.
